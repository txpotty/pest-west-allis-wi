Are Pests Causing Issues? Here's How to Tell.

Recognizing the signs of a pest infestation early can be crucial for minimizing damage and preventing the problem from escalating. Many pests are masters of hiding, but they often leave behind tell-tale clues. Being observant and knowing what to look for can help you catch an infestation before it becomes a major problem. If you notice any of these signs, it's time to call in the professionals at West Allis Pest Pros.

  • Signs of Rodents (Mice, Rats, Squirrels):

    Droppings that look like small pellets, gnaw marks on wood or wiring, scratching sounds in walls or ceilings (especially at night), visible nests made of shredded materials, and a musky odor are all indicators of a rodent problem. Rodents can spread diseases and cause significant structural damage by chewing through electrical wires and plumbing. Our rodent control services are designed to safely and effectively remove rodents and prevent their return.

  • Signs of Ants:

    Trails of ants marching in and out of your home, ant nests near foundations or in walls, and visible ants on countertops or in pantries are clear signs of an ant infestation. Ants are attracted to food sources and can contaminate food preparation areas. We offer comprehensive ant control solutions to eliminate colonies and create barriers to prevent re-entry.

  • Signs of Cockroaches:

    Seeing cockroaches (especially at night), finding cockroach droppings (which resemble coffee grounds or black pepper), a foul odor, and shed exoskeletons are all signs of a cockroach infestation. Cockroaches carry pathogens and can trigger allergies and asthma attacks. Our cockroach control treatments are effective in eliminating these resilient pests.

  • Signs of Termites:

    Mud tubes on exterior walls, discarded wings near windows or doors, bubbling or peeling paint, and hollow-sounding wood when tapped are all potential signs of a termite infestation. Termites can cause extensive structural damage to your home before being detected. Our termite treatment services are designed to eliminate colonies and protect your valuable property.

  • Signs of Bed Bugs:

    Small reddish-brown stains on bedding or mattresses, itchy bites on your skin, shed bed bug skins, and a musty odor are strong indicators of a bed bug infestation. Bed bugs are notoriously difficult to eliminate without professional help. Our bed bug extermination services utilize effective techniques to eradicate these pests and restore your ability to sleep soundly.

  • Signs of Spiders:

    Excessive cobwebs (especially in undisturbed areas), seeing spiders frequently, and finding spider egg sacs can indicate a spider problem. While most spiders are harmless, some can deliver painful bites. Our spider removal services address spider infestations and help prevent them from returning.

  • Signs of Fleas:

    Excessive scratching in pets, small red bites on your skin (often on ankles), and seeing small, fast-moving insects jumping on carpets or furniture are signs of fleas. Fleas are difficult to control and can quickly infest an entire home. We offer effective flea treatment solutions for your home.

  • Signs of Bees and Wasps:

    Visible nests or hives, increased bee or wasp activity around your property, and finding dead bees or wasps indoors indicate the presence of these stinging insects. While important for the environment, nests near your home can be dangerous. Our bee and wasp removal services safely relocate or eliminate nests, prioritizing your safety.

  • Signs of Mosquitoes:

    Excessive mosquito bites, seeing large numbers of mosquitoes around your property, and standing water sources where mosquitoes breed are clear indicators. Mosquitoes are not only annoying but can also transmit diseases. Our mosquito control services help reduce mosquito populations and allow you to enjoy your outdoor spaces.

  • Signs of Silverfish:

    Finding silverfish (small, silvery, wingless insects) in bathrooms, kitchens, or basements, and noticing damage to paper goods, books, or fabrics are signs of silverfish. Silverfish are attracted to moisture and can cause cosmetic damage. We offer silverfish control to eliminate these unwanted guests.

  • Signs of Ticks:

    Finding ticks on pets or family members after spending time outdoors, especially in wooded or grassy areas, indicates the presence of ticks on your property. Ticks can transmit serious diseases. Our tick control services help reduce tick populations in your yard.

  • Signs of Moths:

    Seeing small moths flying indoors (especially in pantries or closets), finding larvae or pupae in food products or on fabrics, and noticing damage to stored grains or clothing are signs of moths. Different types of moths can infest food or fabrics. We provide moth control solutions tailored to the specific type of moth infestation.

  • Signs of Wildlife Issues (Squirrels, Raccoons, etc.):

    Hearing noises in the attic, walls, or chimney (especially at night), discovering damage to roofs, vents, or siding, and seeing wildlife entering or leaving your property are indicators of a wildlife problem. Wildlife can cause significant property damage and pose health risks. Our squirrel removal, raccoon removal, and general wildlife control services humanely remove wildlife and implement exclusion methods.

Beyond these specific issues, any unexplained bites, unusual odors, or strange noises could also indicate a pest infestation. It's always better to investigate and address a potential problem early than to wait for it to become a significant issue. Our team is here to help you identify the problem and implement the most effective solution.

Structural Damage from Pests

Pests like termites and rodents can cause significant structural damage to your home or business. Termites can silently eat away at wooden structures, compromising the integrity of your building. Rodents can gnaw on wiring, increasing the risk of electrical fires, and damage insulation and structural elements. Addressing these pests quickly is vital to protecting your investment and ensuring the safety of your property. Regular inspections can help detect these issues early. The cost of repairing pest-related structural damage can be substantial, far outweighing the cost of preventative pest control.

Health Risks Associated with Pests

Many pests carry and transmit diseases, bacteria, and allergens. Cockroaches and rodents can contaminate food and surfaces, potentially spreading illnesses. Mosquitoes and ticks can transmit serious diseases through their bites. Bed bugs can cause itchy and irritating bites, leading to discomfort and potential secondary infections. Ignoring a pest problem can expose your family or employees to unnecessary health risks. Our pest control methods prioritize the health and safety of our clients by effectively eliminating disease-carrying pests.
